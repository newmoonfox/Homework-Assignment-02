About Me
My name is Vetou. I'm a first generation Cambodian-Chinese-American and I was born in Philly! I like to see myself as a creative person who is interested in the human condition and code. I love going on excursions for the tastiest foods and I'm always up for a challenge. I originally majored in Psychology and minored in Criminology at Penn State, but I'm very excited to learn how to code. I am inspired to apply my qualities and interests in the tech industry through the Penn’s LPS Coding Boot Camp in the hopes to make a social impact through technology.
