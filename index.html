About Her
Vetou Meas is a first generation Cambodian-Chinese-American. Born and raised Philly, she is a creative person who is interested in the human condition and code. Often recognized with a big appetite and love for a challenge, Vetou loves going on excursions for the tastiest foods. She also originally majored in Psychology and minored in Criminology at Penn State, but is very enthusiastic to learn how to code. She is inspired to apply her qualities and interests in the tech industry hope that through the Penn’s LPS Coding Boot Camp she can hope to make a social impact through technology.
